SOURCE NOTES FOR PROFESSIONAL REVIEW

This demo should be reviewed against current Australian requirements before real use.

Useful reference areas:
- Safe Work Australia psychosocial hazards guidance: high job demands, low support, poor organisational change management, traumatic material and role clarity can be psychosocial hazards.
- Queensland WorkSafe code of practice for managing psychosocial hazards at work.
- Psychology Board of Australia professional competencies include self-care to sustain professional functioning and wellbeing.
- Fair Work Ombudsman / relevant award or employment contract should be checked for break and employment entitlement rules.
- OAIC health privacy guidance should be checked before real client or staff health information is entered.

This demo does not certify compliance.
