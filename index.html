Database Description for Irene

What the database is for

The database is not for replacing clinical judgement. It is a safety and operations layer that helps Irene see continuity, workload, handovers, leave cover, appointment gaps and follow-up responsibilities.

Suggested tables

ClientsClient ID, initials, preferred clinician, backup clinician, usual frequency, next appointment, care-gap status.
StaffRole, capacity, booked sessions, leave dates, high-support caseload, overload status.
AppointmentsDate, clinician, client ID, status, cancellation reason, rebooked date.
HandoversFrom clinician, to clinician, reason, checklist, date completed, manager sign-off.
AlertsGreen/amber/red/urgent flag, reason, action required, owner, due date, closed date.
Audit LogWho changed what, when, why. Important for evidence, insurance and accountability.

Privacy boundary

Real client data would be sensitive health information. A live version should minimise data, use consent/access rules, keep role-based permissions, and be reviewed before use.

How Genevieve Can Help Irene

Practice benefits

  • Shows clients with no next appointment.
  • Flags repeated cancellations and care gaps.
  • Balances caseloads and high-support clients across staff.
  • Prepares leave cover before a clinician goes away.
  • Creates clearer handovers.
  • Helps admin see what must be rebooked.
  • Creates evidence of action taken.
  • Supports staff wellbeing by showing overload earlier.
  • Improves client continuity and reduces scrambling.
  • Gives Irene a calmer owner/manager view of the whole practice.
