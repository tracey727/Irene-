Dear Irene,

I wanted to show you something I have built with care, respect, and genuine appreciation for the work you and your team have been doing.

I have updated the demo so the title feels more supportive and less confronting.

The new name is:

IRENE Practice Flow & Staff Support Assistant
Powered by GENEVIEVE App™
DEMO ONLY — fake data only

This is not intended as criticism of you, your staff, or the practice. I know your staff are amazing, and they have helped me more than anyone would ever imagine. I also understand that a psychology practice carries heavy emotional work, appointment pressure, cancellations, staff illness, leave, surgery cover, admin load, urgent client needs and constant decision-making.

The reason I made this is because I want Genevieve to help protect the people who are helping us.

This demo is designed to sit alongside workers, not over them. It helps staff tick off what they need during the day, such as:

- break started
- break finished
- admin buffer taken
- debrief/support needed
- manager support needed
- handover complete
- next client covered
- urgent care-gap action completed

The purpose is to make invisible pressure visible before staff become overloaded.

The system can show Irene, or someone Irene chooses, where help is needed:

- which staff have missed a break
- which staff have too many high-support clients
- which staff have too many sessions in a day
- who needs admin time
- who needs a debrief after difficult sessions
- who is carrying repeated cancellations or rebooking pressure
- which clients have no next appointment
- which handovers are incomplete
- where leave cover is not safe yet

The colour system is simple:

GREEN — covered, supported, stable
AMBER — check soon, break due, support may be needed
RED — action needed today, workload or care-gap risk
BLACK / URGENT — immediate safety pathway

I also added a settings area so Irene or someone she trusts can tailor the system to the practice. For example, the practice can adjust maximum sessions per day, high-support caseload limits, break reminder timing, admin buffer time, debrief requirements, leave cover warning days, care-gap timing and support escalation rules.

Genevieve can also learn from data over time. It can notice patterns like breaks being missed repeatedly, a clinician carrying too many high-support clients, admin overload causing rebooking gaps, certain days becoming unsafe, leave periods creating care gaps, staff requesting support more often, and clients being passed around without a completed handover.

This could help Irene save time, reduce brain load, support insurance/risk evidence, improve staff wellbeing, improve client continuity, and create better quality across the practice.

Most importantly, this is not about blaming anyone. It is about creating a supportive system so staff can keep helping people without being drained, forgotten, or overloaded.

The demo uses fake data only. It is not live clinical software and does not contain real client or staff information. A real version would need Irene’s approval, privacy/legal review, clinical governance review, staff consultation, secure access controls, audit logs and professional advice before any real information is used.

I made this because I care and because I can see how hard everyone is trying.

With respect,

Tracey Kennedy
